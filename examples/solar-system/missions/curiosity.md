# Curiosity Rover

Curiosity is a car-sized #rover that landed in Gale Crater on August 6, 2012. An #active #nasa #mission and part of the #mars-science-laboratory program, its primary goal is to investigate whether Mars ever had conditions favorable for microbial life.

## Key Discoveries

- Evidence of ancient riverbeds and lake systems in Gale Crater
- Detection of organic molecules in Martian rocks
- Seasonal methane variations in the atmosphere
- Radiation levels measured for future human mission planning

Curiosity's findings on [[planets/mars.md|Mars]] have been critical in building the case that the planet was once habitable.
