# Mars

Mars is the fourth #planet from the Sun — a #terrestrial #inner-planet and the primary #exploration-target for human spaceflight. Its reddish appearance comes from iron oxide (rust) on its surface. It has the largest volcano in the solar system (Olympus Mons) and a canyon system (Valles Marineris) that dwarfs the Grand Canyon.
