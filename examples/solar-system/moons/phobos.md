# Phobos

Phobos is the larger and innermost of Mars's two moons — an #irregular #moon likely originating as a #captured-asteroid. It orbits closer to its parent planet than any other known moon in the Solar System — just 6,000 km above the Martian surface. It completes an orbit in under 8 hours, faster than Mars rotates.
