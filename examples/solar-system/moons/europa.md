# Europa

Europa is the smallest of Jupiter's four Galilean moons — an #icy #moon and the sixth-largest satellite in the Solar System. Beneath its crust lies a #subsurface-ocean containing roughly twice the water of all of Earth's oceans combined, making it a top target for #astrobiology research.

Europa is considered one of the most likely places in the solar system to find extraterrestrial life due to its liquid water ocean, energy sources from tidal heating, and potential chemical nutrients.
