# The Sun

The Sun is the #star at the center of the Solar System. It is a nearly perfect ball of hot plasma, heated to incandescence by nuclear fusion reactions in its core. Classified as a #g-type #main-sequence star (spectral type G2V), it has been burning hydrogen for about 4.6 billion years and is roughly halfway through its main-sequence lifespan.
