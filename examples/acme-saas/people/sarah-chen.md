# Sarah Chen

Sarah is the #tech-lead of the [[teams/platform.md|Platform team]]. She joined Acme in early 2023 after 8 years at Stripe, mostly on their payments infrastructure. She has strong opinions about #api-design (everything should be idempotent, every endpoint should have rate limiting from day one) and has been pushing to get the tech debt under control since she arrived.

She's currently the driving force behind the [[projects/auth-migration.md|OAuth2 migration]] — she wrote the original RFC and has been coordinating across teams to get it done. She was also the incident commander during the [[incidents/2025-02-14-pipeline-outage.md|Valentine's Day pipeline outage]], which she's still a little salty about because the root cause was a known issue she'd flagged in Q3 2024.
